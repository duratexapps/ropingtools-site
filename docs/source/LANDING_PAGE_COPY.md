## Four tool cards

### 1. Coaching → `/coaching` (live)
**Tag:** For Ropers
**Title:** Get Coached. Actually.
**Body:** A complete, integrated roping course — swing mechanics to mental game — built around one coherent system instead of a stack of contradictory tips from ten different clinics. Upload your own video and get real, specific feedback on your loop, your delivery, and your position. Try the first chapter free.
**CTA:** Start Learning →

### 2. Steer Me → `/steerme` (live)
**Tag:** For Ropers & Producers
**Title:** Skip the Draw. Keep the Check.
**Body:** Find and lock in your own partner ahead of time — no draw-in fee, no luck of the draw. Producers get entries who show up ready to pay, not owing it out of their winnings. And coming soon: tap straight through to the venue to lock down your RV spot and stalls before you ever hit the road.
**CTA:** Find Your Partner →

### 3. Two Minute Stall → `twominutestall.com` (live, external, opens new tab)
**Tag:** For Ropers on the Road
**Title:** Stalled in Two Minutes. Gone When You Are.
**Body:** A patent-pending, panel-free stalling system that mounts straight to your trailer — no bulky panels, no tangled rope, no wasted storage. Deploy it, use it, stow it, and get back on the road.
**CTA:** See Two Minute Stall →

### 4. Draw Pro → `/drawpro` (coming soon)
**Tag:** For Producers & Back Office
**Title:** Coming Soon
**Body:** The one-stop way to run your draw and notify every entrant — without printing a sheet, cutting a slip, or chasing down contestants by hand. Draw Pro is built to take the draw-day scramble off your plate entirely. It's not live yet — but Steer Me already eliminates the need for a draw on a huge share of your entries today. Start there while Draw Pro finishes the rest.
