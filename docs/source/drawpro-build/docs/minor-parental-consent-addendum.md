# Draw Pro — Minor & Parental Consent Addendum (DRAFT)

> **This is a starting draft, not final legal language.** Team roping fields
> routinely include entrants under 18. This addendum exists because that fact
> changes both the legal handling of entrant data and the entry-form UX itself —
> have counsel confirm the age threshold and consent mechanism against applicable
> state and federal law (including COPPA if any entrant may be under 13) before
> this is finalized. Placeholders are marked `[ ]`.

**Applies to:** any entrant under the age of 18 (or the age of majority in their
jurisdiction, if different — placeholder `[ ]`).

---

## 1. Who Enters a Minor's Information

A minor may **not** submit their own entry through Draw Pro's self-service entry
form. A parent or legal guardian must be the one who:

- Creates or uses the account (or guest entry) the minor's entry is submitted under
- Enters the minor's classification number, contact information, and role
- Agrees to the Terms of Service and this Addendum on the minor's behalf

**Product implication:** the entry form should include an age/minor declaration
step before allowing submission, rather than relying on the Terms of Service alone
to catch this after the fact. This isn't yet reflected in the entry form built so
far and should be added before public launch.

## 2. Whose Contact Information Is Used for Notifications

The email address used to notify a minor entrant of their team number and partner
should be the **parent or guardian's** email, not the minor's own — even if the
minor has their own email address. This keeps Draw Pro from directly texting or
emailing a minor without a parent in the loop.

**Product implication:** the entry form should not assume the email field is the
entrant's own address when a minor is being entered — worth a distinct "parent/
guardian contact" field in that flow rather than reusing the standard entrant email
field as-is.

## 3. Scanned Cards Involving Minors

Where a minor's information is collected via a scanned handwritten card (Option 1),
the same image-retention and OCR-handling rules in the Privacy Policy apply, with no
different treatment for minors' card images at this time — this is a placeholder;
counsel may recommend additional handling (e.g., shorter retention) specifically
for minors' scanned data.

## 4. What We Do Not Do

- We do not knowingly allow a minor to create their own RopingTools account or
  submit their own entry unaccompanied by a parent/guardian's account or consent.
- We do not send SMS or email notifications directly to a minor's own contact
  information via Draw Pro; notifications go to the parent/guardian contact on file.
- We do not use a minor's classification history or entry data for any purpose
  beyond running the draw and event notifications.

## 5. If a Minor's Information Was Entered Incorrectly

A parent or guardian may contact [RopingTools support contact — placeholder] to
correct or remove a minor's information from Draw Pro at any time, subject to the
event's own entry-correction deadlines set by the producer.

## 6. Producer Responsibility

Producers using Draw Pro to run an event with minor entrants are responsible for
their own compliance with any age-related eligibility rules, waivers, or
sanctioning-body requirements specific to their event — Draw Pro's role here is
limited to how the software itself collects and handles a minor's data, not the
event's own participation rules.
